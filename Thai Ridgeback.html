Thai Ridgeback

 

Appearance : Thai Ridgebacks have a strong, well-proportioned physique and are medium-sized canines. A distinctive characteristic of theirs is a ridge of hair that runs up their backs opposite the direction that the rest of their coat does. Either long or short hair can be found along the ridge, and it usually stands up straight.

Colour: They are available in a range of hues, including as fawn, blue, black, and red. There are Thai Ridgebacks that are multicoloured.

Tail and Ears: A Thai Ridgeback may have fully or partially erect ears. The tail is not curled; it is medium length and slightly bent.

Eyes: Medium-sized, almond-shaped, and usually brown, Thai Ridgebacks have eyes.

 

Size and Weight: Male Thai Ridgebacks average 22-24 inches at the shoulder, while females typically stand 20-22 inches. Thai Ridgebacks are medium-sized canines. Typically, they weigh 35 to 75 pounds.

Temperament: Thai Ridgebacks are renowned for their intelligence, loyalty, and protectiveness. They might be incredibly loving with their family but are frequently reticent among outsiders. They are natural hunters with a strong hunting drive. To guarantee that they are well-mannered and well-behaved friends, they require constant training and socializing.

Exercise and Activity: Due to their high energy levels, Thai Ridgebacks need a lot of mental and physical stimulation. They take pleasure in jogging, trekking, and fetch games.

Health: Although hip dysplasia, elbow dysplasia, and dermoid sinus (a skin ailment) are rare health problems that affect Thai Ridgebacks, overall their health is good. It's critical for owners to monitor their dog's health and schedule routine veterinary examinations.

Lifespan: A Thai Ridgeback's typical lifespan is twelve to fifteen years.


Grooming: The short, silky coat of a Thai Ridgeback is simple to maintain. Regular brushing might help prevent loose hair because they do shed. In order to preserve their general health and wellbeing, simple grooming procedures including nail clipping, ear cleaning, and dental care are also critical.
